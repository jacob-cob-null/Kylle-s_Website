ABOUT US

Kylles began in 2020 during the pandemic as a humble home-based venture to earn extra income. Starting with baked breads, we relied on social media to connect with our customers and grow our online presence. Now, driven by a deep passion for cooking, I’ve turned that small dream into something bigger—officially launching Kylles Kitchenette in 2024.
